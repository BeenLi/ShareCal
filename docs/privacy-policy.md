# OurDays Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

OurDays is a private couple calendar app. The app helps two invited users view selected calendars side by side, share event invitations, and comment on shared events.

## Data Used by the App

OurDays may access the calendars that you explicitly select after granting calendar permission. Depending on your privacy setting, shared calendar mirrors may include event title, time, location, notes, URL, source calendar name, and calendar color.

OurDays may also store app-created invitations, comments, read state, and basic member profile information such as display name, avatar color, and an iCloud account identifier needed for CloudKit sharing.

## How Data Is Stored

OurDays stores data locally on your device and in Apple iCloud using CloudKit so that selected information can sync between you and your invited partner. Shared data is visible only to the users included in the CloudKit share.

OurDays does not operate an independent server and does not sell personal data.

## Tracking and Advertising

OurDays does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Data Sharing

Shared calendar mirrors, invitations, and comments are shared only with the partner you invite through iCloud sharing. Data may also be processed by Apple as part of iCloud, App Store, and device operating system services.

## Deleting Data

You can stop using OurDays by deleting the app from your device. You can also stop sharing by removing the iCloud share. Calendar events created in your local Calendar remain under your control in Apple Calendar.

For support, use the OurDays support page.
